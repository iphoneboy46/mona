WHOWEARE

Step by step overcoming difficulties and challenges, Great Hair has become a prestigious symbol in the beauty industry with more than 15 years of solid experience. Founded in 2008, Great Hair is more than just a wig company, it is a model of patience, creativity and commitment to quality.

Crafting Quality Wigs
from Vietnamese Hair

At its headquarters in the dynamic city, Great Hair focuses on manufacturing high-class wig products from human hair materials originating from Vietnam - a country famous for its high quality hair source. By ensuring quality and durable raw materials, Great Hair has set a new standard for smoothness, shine and naturalness for wigs.
