About Us

BEST SAAS is a leading subsidiary of BEST INC, a prestigious company listed on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE). With over 15 years of experience in the industry, BEST SAAS has established itself as a trusted provider of cutting-edge technology and AI-powered solutions to businesses, particularly in the ecommerce and warehouse sectors.


Since 2017, BEST SAAS has embarked on a journey of global expansion, successfully entering markets worldwide. Today, our services are available in numerous countries, including the United States, Italy, Australia, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia, and Australia. We take pride in serving a diverse clientele and supporting businesses across borders.


BEST SAAS is a software as a services solution that provided an automates & intelligent multichannel e-commerce selling processes & warehouse management system. BEST SAAS are an industry leading, highest performing and flexible ecommerce solution provider in Asia.


As an industry leader in ecommerce multi-channel solutions, we understand the challenges you face in managing multiple sales channels effectively. Our state-of-the-art software enables you to effortlessly sell and manage your products across various platforms, including popular marketplaces like Lazada, Shopee, TikTok, Qo10 and Shopify. With our intelligent AI inventory control, you can optimize stock levels and prevent stockouts, ensuring seamless order fulfilment and customer satisfaction.
